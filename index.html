Second Chance Plants

Giving new life to plants and trees destined for the trash — showing that with a little care, even what's been discarded can thrive again.

About Us

Second Chance Plants is a nonprofit dedicated to rescuing unsellable plants from nurseries and wholesalers — plants that would otherwise be thrown away. We use them to beautify communities, teach environmental responsibility, and inspire hope through regeneration.

How It Works

  • We partner with plant wholesalers to receive donated, unsellable plants.
  • Our team and volunteers revive and rehome the plants.
  • We deliver plants to schools, memorial gardens, urban spaces, and more.

Get Involved

Whether you're a plant lover, student, teacher, or business, there’s a place for you in our mission. You can volunteer, donate, sponsor a project, or help us spread the word.
